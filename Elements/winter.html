Winter

Finding your inner peace during the winter season will be very different compared to the other seasons due to the uniquely different landscapes. Snow is an excellent sign of tranquility. Enjoy as it gently sweeps over the landscape. While it may be chillingly cold, it can wake you up to the beauty of your workd turned white. Lakes and ponds freeeze over giving you a mirror to the sky. Winter can be a difficult and cold season but with the right mindset it can truly be a real winter wonderland.
