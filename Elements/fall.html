Fall

The season of fall has the largest amount of color in the foliage. Views from higher altutudes become awe inspiring as mountain sides become giant canvases for red, orange and yellow folliage as intense as fire. Enjoy the cooling weather with leaves of changeing colors gliding gently around you to the ground, blanketing it for the cold winter weather, and bringing a crisp new scent to the air. Fall is your last chance to enjoy outdoor activities before the winter frost so make the best of it.
